# Prompt for Proof Reading
Act as an expert academic copyeditor specializing in IT, Software Development, and Model Engineering.
Please proofread and edit the following section of my master's thesis. My primary goal is to ensure the text is polished, clear, and adheres to standard academic conventions.


Please focus on the following:
Mechanics: Correct any spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors.
Academic Tone: Ensure the tone is formal, objective, and appropriate for a master's level computer science/IT thesis.
Clarity and Flow: Improve sentence structure for better readability. Break up overly long or confusing sentences, and ensure smooth transitions between paragraphs.
Technical Accuracy: Preserve all specific software development and model engineering terminology. Do not alter the technical meaning of the text.

Important Constraints:
Do not change my core arguments, data, or underlying meaning.
If a sentence is highly confusing and you are unsure of my intent, highlight it and offer a couple of different ways to rewrite it based on what you think I mean.

Output format:
Please provide only the parts you would change.
Below the text, provide a brief bulleted list explaining any major structural changes you made or areas where I should double-check my phrasing
